Photonics

We develop integrated photonics systems end-to-end: from design and layout, through fabrication and device testing, all the way to packaging and integration.

We are interested in working on: photonic systems at both telecom and visible wavelengths, in Si, SiN, and TFLN; electro-optic modulators and acousto-optic modulators for quantum applications; advanced packaging and coupling between heterogenous quantum systems.
